Basic Ideas:
- 2D game
- Trash collector
- Don't let the trash hit you
- Some trash you don't want
- There are 20 levels
- Every 1000 points, the level increments
- Each time the level is incremented, more trash will be spawned
- To increase health, there will be health drops spawning every level, but not many

Types of Trash:
-- Good
- Paper
- Cardboard
- Wood
- Water Bottles
- Soda Cans
- Candy Wrapper
- Scrap Plastic
- Scrap Metal
- Broken Electronics
 
-- Bad
- Yard trimmings
- Cigarette Butts
- Food scraps
- Broken Glass
